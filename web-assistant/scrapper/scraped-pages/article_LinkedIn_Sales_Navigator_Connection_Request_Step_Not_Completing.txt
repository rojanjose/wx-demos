LinkedIn Sales Navigator Connection Request Step Not Completing

Mar 24, 2023•Knowledge
Package Availability
Indicates which SalesLoft package(s) include the feature referenced in this knowledge article.
Content

If you have previously connected with a Person on LinkedIn or have previously sent a connection request that is still in pending, you will not be able to complete LinkedIn Sales Navigator Connection Request Steps.

When this happens, skip the Person to the next step in the Cadence.
