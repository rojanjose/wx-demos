Analytics Overview: Calls Results Panel

Sep 23, 2022•Knowledge

Content
The Calls Results panel provides a quick insight into the logged call activity based on your Analytics Filters. 

The panel looks at the the total calls logged for the selected date range as well as the average calls logged per day. 

Taking these details into consideration, the panel aims to answer the following questions: 
Are we logging calls?
Are we making enough calls?
Are we hitting our call activity targets?
In this article, you will learn about the Call Results panel, including:
The Call Results Panel
Call Results Metrics and Calculations
Panel Drill Down: Calls Report
The Call Results Panel
The Call Results panel contains the following details from the top of the panel working our way down:
Panel Name: Call Results
View Report: (Drill down to more details) Takes you to the Calls Standard Report.  
Primary Metric: Total Calls Logged
Primary Metric Chart: Below the Total Calls Logged, the chart shows the visual representation of the primary metric. 
Secondary Metrics Include:
Average Calls Logged Per Day
Voicemails
Connected Calls
Positive Calls: Connected Calls are determined by any calls labeled with the disposition Connected. Then, whenever calls are logged with the sentiments Interested, Timeline 6-12 Months, or Demo Scheduled, those calls are added to the number of positive calls.
Period Comparison based on date selection: To the right of the metrics, you will see a small percentage in either green or red. This will show how your numbers have increased or decreased over time. 
 
Call Results Metrics and Calculations
Each panel contains primary and secondary metrics. These metrics are calculated in different ways. Check out the Analytics Metric Calculations for a complete list of metrics. 

The Calls Results  panel includes the following metrics based on the associated calculations. 
 
Metrics
	
Calculations

 Total Calls Logged	 Sum(Calls Logged)
 Average Calls Logged Per Day 	 Sum(Calls Logged)/Count(Days)
 
Panel Drill Down: Calls Report
 This panel drills down into the Calls Standard Report, where you can see a detailed record of each rep's call activity.
