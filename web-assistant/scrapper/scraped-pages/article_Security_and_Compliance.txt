At Salesloft, we take security and privacy seriously. We understand that in gaining your business, we are forming a relationship based on trust, and we intend to maintain and build upon that trust.

To help ensure the security, privacy, integrity, and availability of your company's information, Salesloft partners with industry-leading technology providers and has implemented robust security and risk-management practices.

Please visit the Salesloft Trust Portal for an overview of our program and to access to confidential documentation, including the following: 

Our SOC 2 Type 2 attestation report
ISO 27001 certification
Other independent assessments
Company policies and standards related to security, privacy and compliance

Things to Note: You will need to request access prior to viewing the information contained in the Trust Portal.
