My User's Conversations Trial Has Expired, But The Conversation Recordings Toggle is still enabled. What Does The User Still Have Access To?

Aug 9, 2023•Knowledge
Package Availability
Indicates which SalesLoft package(s) include the feature referenced in this knowledge article.
Content

The Conversations Recording toggle simultaneously grants access to conference recording and call recording. When the trial ends, your user will no longer have access to Conversations to view and manage recordings; however, they will be able to record calls.
