Analytics Overview: LinkedIn Results Panel

Jul 18, 2022•Knowledge
Package Availability
Indicates which SalesLoft package(s) include the feature referenced in this knowledge article.
Content
The LinkedIn panel shows the amount of LinkedIn completed steps as well as the Linked activity types based on your Analytics Filters. 

In addition to the details in this panel, you can view LinkedIn data from the the Process Standard Report, Team Standard Report, Accounts Standard Report, and Cadences Standard Report.

Taking these details into consideration, the panel aims to answer the following questions:
Are we increasing our LinkedIn cadence efforts?
What types of LinkedIn actions are we completing?
Which teams or groups are increasing their LinkedIn activity?
How are our LinkedIn steps trending? 
What is the breakdown of LinkedIn actions by cadence or by user?
In this article, you will learn about the LinkedIn panel, including:
The LinkedIn Results Panel
LinkedIn Metrics and Calculations
Panel Drill Down: Team Process - LinkedIn Report
The LinkedIn Results Panel
The LinkedIn panel contains the following details from the top of the panel working our way down:
Panel Name: LinkedIn Results
View Report: (Drill down to more details) Takes you to LinkedIn specific version of the Team Standard Report.  
Primary Metric: LinkedIn Completed Steps: The total number of LinkedIn completed steps.
Primary Metric Chart: The trend and average lines show LinkedIn completed steps for the selected date range.
Secondary Metrics include: 
LinkedIn Research: The completed LinkedIn steps that are research.
LinkedIn Introductions: The completed LinkedIn steps that are introductions.
LinkedIn Connections: The completed LinkedIn steps that are connections.
LinkedIn InMail: The completed LinkedIn steps that are InMail.
Period Comparison based on date selection: To the right of the metrics, you will see a small percentage in either green or red. This will show how your numbers have increased or decreased over time. 
 
LinkedIn Metrics and Calculations
Each panel contains primary and secondary metrics. These metrics are calculated in different ways. Check out the Analytics Metric Calculations for a complete list of metrics. 

The LinkedIn panel includes the following metrics based on the associated calculations. 
Metrics
	
Calculations

Completed Steps	 The total number of all LinkedIn completed steps.
LinkedIn Research	 Sum of LinkedIn steps that are research.
LinkedIn Introductions	 Sum of LinkedIn steps that are introductions.
 LinkedIn Connections	 Sum of LinkedIn steps that are connections.
 LinkedIn InMail	 Sum of LinkedIn steps that are InMail.
 
Panel Drill Down: Team Process - LinkedIn Report

This panel drills down into a modified version of the Team Standard Report that only has LinkedIn results. This report includes the following columns: 

LinkedIn Completed Steps
LinkedIn Research
LinkedIn Connections
LinkedIn InMail
LinkedIn Introductions
Skipped Integration Steps
Overdue Integration Steps
On-time Integration Steps
