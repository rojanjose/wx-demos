Send an Email with Snippets

Apr 10, 2023•Knowledge

Content
Snippets can be added to any email you send using Salesloft. You can compose a new email and include a quick snippet that contains a case study, or add a snippet containing information to a previously created template.  

To insert a snippet into your emails, follow these steps:
Open an email window to send an Email.
In the lower right corner of the email window, select the + Insert button.
At the top of the Insert window, select the Snippets tab.
Choose My Snippets, Team Snippets, or you can search for a specific snippet.  
Select the snippet.
Hit Insert. The snippet will populate in the email where your cursor is located.
Send your email.
