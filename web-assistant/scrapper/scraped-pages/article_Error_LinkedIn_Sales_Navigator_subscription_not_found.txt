Error: "LinkedIn Sales Navigator subscription not found"

Mar 24, 2023•Knowledge

Content
Another error you may encounter is the "LinkedIn Sales Navigator subscription not found" message. There are several methods for addressing this error, including:
Confirm your license: Reach out to LinkedIn Sales Navigator admins to confirm you have the appropriate license. For more information, review the LinkedIn Sales Navigator plans.
Check the LinkedIn primary email setting:
In LinkedIn, navigate to Me.
Click Settings & Privacy.
Select Account Access/Email.
Confirm that the corporate email address for the subscription is set as the primary email address.
Deauthorize the LinkedIn app:
In LinkedIn, navigate to Me.
Click Settings & Privacy.
Select Data Privacy.
Select Other Applications.
Next to Permitted Services, choose Change.
Remove the Navigator Widget Production App.
Return to Salesloft, clear your cookies, and reattempt the LinkedIn Navigator steps. You will need to reauthorize and confirm the connected LinkedIn user seat.
Contact LinkedIn: If none of the other suggested fixes solve the issue, reach out to the LinkedIn support team to confirm licensing and eliminate problems with duplicate seats, license reassignments, etc.
