Email communication is essential to the sales workplace, and effective email communication starts with sending emails to real people. 

You can use email validation resources to verify the contacts in your email list. By validating your email list, you can send emails confidently and ensure that the emails you send land in an inbox.

In this article, you will learn about and access a list of email validation resources, including:
ZeroBounce
Bouncer
Xverify
NeverBounce
BriteVerify
Email Industries
ZeroBounce
ZeroBounce is an online email validation platform that removes invalid, inactive and spam-trap emails in bulk. 
Bouncer
Bouncer is an email verification API that verifies and validates your email list. 
Xverify
Xverify is an email verification tool that uses data intelligence to improve email deliverability, monitor IP blacklists, filter out spam and more. 
NeverBounce
NeverBounce offers email deliverability solutions such as high-risk detection, and instant bounce analysis. 
BriteVerify
BriteVerify helps you maintain a clean database by validating contact information, including international phone numbers and mailing addresses. 
Email Industries
Email Industries provides email marketing and deliverability solutions such as removing spam, and validating email addresses in your contact list.
