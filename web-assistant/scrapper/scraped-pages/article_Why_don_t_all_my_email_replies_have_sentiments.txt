Why don't all my email replies have sentiments?

Mar 28, 2023•Knowledge
Package Availability
Indicates which SalesLoft package(s) include the feature referenced in this knowledge article.
Content

Email sentiment replies that are Lofted are marked with sentiments; other types of replies will not be marked with a sentiment.

Lofted emails include those sent directly from the Salesloft app and emails sent using Connect for Salesloft's Lofted button. 

Email activity sync will not have an attached email sentiment for an email reply.
